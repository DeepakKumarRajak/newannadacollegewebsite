  Welcome To Annada College, Hazaribagh


“Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.” Malcolm X

Goals are to aspire for and persevere till the end. Blest are those who walk down their career path carrying a remembrance of never tarnishing glorious alma mater like Annada College, Hazaribagh. The college was established on July 09 th , 1979 marking the centenary celebration of the establishment of Annada Shishu Vidyalaya, Hazaribagh. Knowledge is not to be limited by the false stakes in society; be it in the name of community, personal or family background or mode of earning livelihood. Annada College, Hazaribagh leaps beyond these superficial social obstructions and holds the pride of being the only Permanently Affiliated Linguistic (Bangla) Minority Undergraduate College of Jharkhand.
